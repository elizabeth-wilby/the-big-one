Coding Projects

Over the last year, as I've advanced as both a writer and a developer, I've become interested in finding ways to merge the two so it never felt like I was splitting my brain in half. I decided to build two websites. (One for each book) These include outlines and thoughts as well as projects showing maps, timelines, cards for characters and monsters, ones identifying the themes I've focused on, and even a small game. Some of these are still works in progress, but they have given me room to experiment and learn creatively and technically. This website shows some of that coming to life.

Creation & Authority

If God won't act, can man act in His place? Girolamo believes every obstacle removed is proof of God's approval. Right and wrong and acting in desperation are meant to be judged by the reader.

Sacred vs. Profane

Saints' relics stolen and reassembled. Not symbols but actual flesh and blood, stolen and repurposed for reassembly into one being. Creation or desecration?

Obedience & Violence

Murder a corrupt Pope to serve God. Obedience to higher authority requires disobedience to earthly power. Does serving God absolve that responsibility?

Certainty as Horror

Girolamo never doubts or questions himself. His certainty is absolute the way he views God's bestowed athority on himself.
